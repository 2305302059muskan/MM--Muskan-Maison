Shipping Policy

We offer reliable and swift delivery across India. Every Muskan Maison order is packaged with care in our signature gift wrapping.


  • Free shipping on all orders above ₹2,999
  • Standard delivery: 5–7 business days
  • Express delivery: 2–3 business days (₹199 extra)
  • Same-day delivery available in Bengaluru (₹299)
  • Orders placed before 12pm ship the same day
  • Tracking link shared via email and SMS
🔄

Return Policy

We want you to love your MM purchase. If you're not completely satisfied, we make returns simple and stress-free.


  • Returns accepted within 14 days of delivery
  • Item must be unused and in original packaging
  • Tags and dust bag must be intact
  • Customised products are non-returnable
  • Sale items can be exchanged but not refunded
  • Free return pickup available in select cities
💳

Refund Policy

Once your return is received and inspected, we process refunds swiftly to your original payment method.


  • Refund initiated within 2 business days of inspection
  • UPI / Wallets: Refund in 1–2 business days
  • Credit / Debit card: 5–7 business days
  • Net banking: 3–5 business days
  • Store credit option available for instant credit
  • Exchange processed within 7–10 business days
✈️

International Orders

We currently ship to select international destinations. Contact us to check availability for your country.


  • Available to UAE, UK, USA, Singapore, and Canada
  • Delivery: 10–14 business days
  • Customs duties are the responsibility of the buyer
  • Minimum order value of ₹8,000 for international
  • Returns on international orders: store credit only
  • Email us for a shipping quote: hello@muskanmaison.in
